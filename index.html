I joined the University of Baltimore in 2013 as Assistant Professor. Currently I am the Program Director for the degree of Applied Information Technology. Before coming to UB, I spent many years at Towson University as Lecturer and Associate Director of the Master's Program in Applied Information Technology. The primary focus of my research revolves around e-learning solutions for programming language education, creating material that students can utilize to learn and review key concepts in multiple programming languages. I also conduct research on applications of fuzzy sets in various fields.
