e-Learning Resources

The following is a curated list of online resources that may be utilized by students to prepare or review concepts for particular courses. The list is meant for students enrolled in the Applied Information Technology program at the University of Baltimore, as the course pairings will match what is required in this program. For a full list of courses and course descriptions in this major you may find that information at the following page: COSC Course Descriptions
